### [GitButler CLI Is Really Good](https://matduggan.com/gitbutler-cli-is-really-good/)

GitButler is new to me. At first glance, the ergonomics look a lot like `jj`.
I don't feel the pain as much with `git`. Sure, I work on different branches all the time, but switching is not a problem, and neither is cherry-picking.
Stacked PRs are more painful, but `git rebase --update-refs` goes a long way. It still requires multiple pushes, but I don't do that often. Worth watching.
